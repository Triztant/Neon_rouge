This platform exists to share music in a more direct and intentional way. The goal is to create a space built around genuine taste, discovery, and personal connection rather than recommendation engines optimised for engagement. Every selection shared on the platform comes from real personal interest — not marketing, playlist placement, label strategy, or trend cycles.

As music discovery has become increasingly shaped by algorithms, sponsored promotion, and short-form virality, much of the experience around finding music has started to feel flattened and disconnected.

This project is a simple alternative: a place where artists, designers, DJs, writers, and others can share ten tracks or sounds that genuinely matter to them, directly with listeners.

There are no rankings, no metrics, and no pressure to perform. Just people passing music on because they believe it is worth hearing.
